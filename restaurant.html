Restaurant and Audit Forms

The forms are designed to simplify Title 4 compliance for Hotel/Motel with Restaurant (series 11) and Restaurant (series 12) licensees. For your convenience, many of the forms can be filled in on the computer. The Business Data Report will be required with all Hotel/Motel with Restaurant(series 11) and Restaurant (series 12) liquor license renewals for licensees that have been in business for 12 months or longer.
